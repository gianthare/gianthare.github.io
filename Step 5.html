    Electronic basic components
    Two type of electronic component: the Passive-responds to the flow of electrical energy and ether store or dissipate energy. The Active supplies energy or controls the flow of energy requiring an external power source to be triggered to amplify electrical signal.

    [Passive Linear Components]
     RESISTOR: resists the flow of current and cause a reduction in potential. The electrical energy is absorbed by the resistor and dissipated in form of heat.
     CAPACITOR: stores electrical charge and opposes the flow of the current when connected in a DC (direct current) circuit.
     INDUCTOR: stores energy in form of a magnetic field. Faraday’s law( a magnetic field is created when current flows through the wire and the electromotive fore developed opposes the applied voltage.

    [Passive Non Linear Componets]
    DIODES: restricts current flow in only one direction with a combination of two differently doped regions, P,N, forming a junction which controls the flow of charge. Ex)Light Emitting Diode(LED), switches

    [Active Electronic Components]
    TRANSISTOR: Transistors are devices which generally transform resistance from one part of the circuit to another. They can be voltage controlled or current controlled. A transistor can work as an amplifier or as a switch.
    For more details: https://www.elprocus.com/basic-components-used-electronics-electrical/
